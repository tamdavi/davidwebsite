About David Tamakloe

Hi, I’m David Tamakloe — a passionate communicator, lifelong learner, and creative at heart.

With a background in Information Studies and Psychology and ongoing studies in ICT and Software Development, I’ve found myself at the unique intersection of tech, human behavior, and storytelling. Whether I’m managing patient records at a medical center, welcoming audiences at Roverman Productions, or singing my heart out on stage, I bring my full self — curious, detail-oriented, and people-focused.

My journey has taken me through healthcare, the arts, government, and broadcast media, where I’ve built experience in data management, customer service, content creation, and event marketing. Along the way, I’ve cultivated a deep appreciation for how systems and stories shape our world.

When I’m not at work, you’ll probably find me writing songs, watching a good film, or just vibing to the next big idea. I believe in doing everything with purpose, joy, and a little bit of rhythm.

Let’s connect — and maybe create something meaningful together.
